About the AI

With L.L.A.V., our new state of the Art AI, we are able to determine whether or not a customer is under the legal drinking age of 21. This helps us ensure that the we are within the guidlines of the law and are able to keep our liquor stores and communities safe.

How it is done

L.L.A.V. takes an image of a subject and is trained to transform and morph the image in order to get a higher understanding of it
